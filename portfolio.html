first project

In truth this represents for me two projects in one, or rather a sort of second portfolio where I can group mine together projects regarding plc programming, in particular for the moment we are talking about siemens plc
The PLC is a programmable electronic device capable of interpreting and executing the instructions of the programs mentioned in it,
interacting with a control circuit through input and output devices.
PLCs are usually used in industrial automation as they are devices resistant to extreme heat conditions, humidity or dust.

second project

>My second project concerns another topic that has always been fascinating to me, the world of coffee.
in this project I would like to talk about my skills, convey this beautiful passion and perhaps share it with someone in the future the tasting of some particular blend or single origin or some 'crazy' composition in latteart.

third project

this project was created for a place that basically simply needs to write its own menu and be able to recall it with the qrcode.
Who knows...Maybe in the future there is a plan on their part to extend the site with events, photos and a community

fourth project

This site is a tribute to my friends who live far away.
Having moved, I wanted to bring the typical fantasy football game that we have been playing for years with friends from paper to online. Maybe one day it could become a real app for enthusiasts..
